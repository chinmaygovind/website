How to play

  • Flip your top card on your turn (press F, or tap your pile).
  • Slap the pile the instant it is slappable (press Space, or the SLAP button):
Double · 7 7 Sandwich · 7 4 7 Top matches bottom Two cards add to 10 King + Queen
  • Face cards: playing J / Q / K / A makes the next player owe 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 cards. Flip a face card and the debt passes on; run out first and whoever laid the last face card wins the pile.
  • Careful: slapping when it is not slappable burns 2 of your cards to the bottom of the pile.
  • Win by collecting the entire deck.
