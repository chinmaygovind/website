How to play

Each monster wants to be the King of Tokyo. Win by reaching 20 victory points, or by being the last monster left alive.

Your turn

Roll six dice up to three times, keeping the ones you like between rolls. Then the faces resolve all at once:

  • 1 / 2 / 3 - three of a kind scores that many victory points (each extra die of the same number adds 1 more).
  • ❤ Heart - heal one damage each. You can't heal while you're in Tokyo.
  • ⚡ Energy - one energy cube each, spent to buy power cards.
  • Claw - deal one damage each. Outside Tokyo you hit whoever's in Tokyo; inside Tokyo you hit everyone else.

Tokyo

If Tokyo is empty you must move in, and you score points for taking it and for starting your turn there. But you can't heal, and everyone will be gunning for you. Get hit and you may yield Tokyo to the attacker.

Power cards

Spend energy in the shop for permanent Keep cards and one-shot effects. Pay 2 energy to sweep the three cards and draw fresh ones.

Winning

First to 20 victory points wins - or be the last monster standing.
